Previous Bioinformatics Site

This earlier site connects to my undergraduate population-genomics work. It was built with Folium to map bird trait statistics from AVONET3 and visualize effective population size trends through deep time. In the plots, the present is on the left side of the x-axis and the Ice Age is farther to the right. The time series were estimated with MSMC2 using avian genomic data from B10K. Squiggles builds on that work as a Dash web app for visualizing and clustering MSMC time-series curves, with k-means models trained on B10K bird data. Related repos: MSMC exploratory analysis, MSMC clustering.
